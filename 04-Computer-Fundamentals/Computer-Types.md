## Overview

Different types of computers are designed for different workloads, performance requirements, and environments.

## Desktop Computers

General-purpose computers designed for home and office use.

## Laptops

Portable computers that integrate all major hardware components into a single device.

## Servers

Computers built to provide services, applications, and resources to multiple clients over a network.

## Smartphones and Tablets

Mobile computers optimized for portability, battery life, and touch interfaces.

## Embedded Systems

Small computers integrated into other devices to perform dedicated tasks, such as routers, vehicles, medical devices, and IoT equipment.

## Mainframes

Highly reliable systems designed to process massive numbers of transactions for large organizations such as banks and governments.
